EMI Landing page

Project overview

A front-end focused web development project where the owner approached me with the idea of a landing page for the company. I began by sketching out wireframes for the site, and showing these to the owner to get their feedback. Once the layout decision was made, I created a mockup of the site in Adobe XD and sent it to the owner for feedback. I then implemented the owner's feedback into the final mockup and developed the site HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JQuery.

Technologies Used

  • Adobe XD
  • HTML/CSS
  • JQuery
  • Bootstrap
