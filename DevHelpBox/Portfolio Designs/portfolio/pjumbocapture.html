JumboCapture Project

Project Overview

A user experience design project for my capstone course that began by defining the problem, followed by preliminary research, interviews, and surveys. From the data gathered, we developed personas to better represent our user group. After the research portion of this project was completed, we began sketching and wireframing. We conducted usability testing on both our sketches and our wireframes to ensure our design choices. After our wireframes were approved we moved into designing our low-fidelity product and followed that up with more usability testing to continue to validate our design. Finally we moved on to designing our final high-fidelity product, and followed this up with one more round of usability testing. We used InVision to develop our functional prototype. More information regarding this project will be added in the near future.
